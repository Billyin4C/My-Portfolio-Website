Bryan C. Bean

I am a 32 year old husband and father of two boys. I have been with the same company (Lance Inc.) for the past 10 years. I have trained, I have managed, and now I currently own and operate my snack distribution company (Bean Distribution LLC). With the majority of my adult life spent in sales, I battled myself and went back to school. Balancing life with my wife and children as well as with school and my business, was quite possibly the toughest thing that I have had to deal with. Thanks to coffee, late nights, and my son's forgiveness of missed soccer games, I just completed my AAS in Computer Programming with a 3.6 from Gaston College. To some that may seem small, but to others in similar situations, we smile together at the perserverance we didn't know that we had and the happiness that came at the end of our accomplishment. .
